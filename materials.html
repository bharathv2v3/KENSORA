Our Standards

Material Excellence Principles

◈

Direct Source Relationships

We work directly with quarries, mills and ateliers — no intermediaries — ensuring provenance, quality control and exclusive access.

✦

Rigorous Quality Testing

All materials undergo physical testing for hardness, slip-resistance, stain-resistance and UV stability before entering our library.

⊹

Sustainable Sourcing

Certified quarries, FSC-certified veneers and organic dye fabrics wherever possible — beauty without compromising the planet.

◉

Exclusive Access for Clients

Many materials in our library are unavailable in the Indian retail market — exclusive to Kensora project clients only.
